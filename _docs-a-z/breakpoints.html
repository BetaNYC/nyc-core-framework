Breakpoint Modifiers

Notice the Modifier column in the table above. Across the Framework, breakpoint modifiers are available for many of our component and utility classes.

A breakpoint modifier has a double-dash and two-letter abbreation representing its breakpoint (e.g., --md for the medium breakpoint). Breakpoint modifiers are always afixed to the end of certain component and utility classes.
